# AI_Templates

1.Linkedin_automation_guide
A Make.com automation that watches AI & Tech news, rewrites it into a polished LinkedIn post using Google Gemini, and delivers it to your inbox every morning — no coding required.

💡 What It Does
Every day at your scheduled time, this workflow:

Pulls the latest articles from a Google News RSS feed (AI startup news by default)
Sends the article title, summary, and link to Gemini 2.5 Flash via the Google AI API
Gemini writes a casual, engagement-focused LinkedIn post in your voice
The finished post lands in your Gmail inbox with the subject: New LinkedIn Post Idea 🚀

You copy. You paste. You post.
